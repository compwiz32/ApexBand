Apex High School Band Sponsorship Program

Support the Apex High School Band by becoming a corporate sponsor! Our sponsorship program runs from July through June each school year and offers local businesses a chance to show their support while gaining valuable community exposure.


Contributions help fund band activities, equipment, and performances, and sponsors receive a range of benefits based on their level of support. Join us in enriching music education and celebrating student talent!
